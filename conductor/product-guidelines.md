# Product Guidelines: Household Management Application

## 1. Prose Style

The application's text and messaging will maintain a **clear and concise** tone. Information will be presented directly, simply, and without unnecessary jargon. The goal is to ensure that users can quickly understand instructions, feedback, and content within the application.

## 2. Brand Messaging

Our brand messaging will **focus on practical benefits and efficiency** for household management. We will highlight how the application directly solves common household management problems, saves users time, and improves their organizational capabilities. The communication will emphasize the tangible advantages and the streamlined experience offered by the application.

## 3. Visual Identity

The application will adopt a **modern and minimalist** visual style. This will be characterized by clean lines, simple and uncluttered interfaces, and a focus on essential elements. The aesthetic will be designed for usability and clarity, promoting a calm and efficient user experience.
